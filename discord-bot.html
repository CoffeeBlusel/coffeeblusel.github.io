Privacy Policy

White Bat collects limited data necessary to provide its functionality. This may include Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and command usage logs.

Message content is not stored unless explicitly required for a specific feature. All data is used solely for the operation, maintenance, and improvement of the bot. Data is not sold, shared, or provided to third parties.

Data may be retained for as long as necessary to provide the bot’s services and may be deleted upon request or when no longer required.
